Peter Mung

Peter is a mechanic who has done interesting projects along the way. He has a passion about cars like no other and set his sight to becoming a master technician at his employer. He has increased his efficieny in his life as well as constantly trying to become the best trainer out in his field. He started his journey about 12 years ago when he initially moved to Seattle, WA. He saved up money to buy his dream car 3 years later, a 2003 Subaru WRX. Due to a lot of car modification, his car reaches 410 horsepower including his turbo. Unfortunately due to poor weather, the car spun out and hit a ditch. He set out in living his dream in this car and decided to do a whole body swap from a yellow Subaru WRX to a silver Subaru Impreza. His knowledge comes from his years of experience as a mechanic and wants to continue to grow. Besides cars his passion is himself and creating a peaceful lifestyle than what he first experience the first half of his life. Whether its eating food or walking at the beach. He is passionate in life with whatever he does.
